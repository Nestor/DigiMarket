How did we meet?

Well it started off as Exus casually checking his twitter, as he does in an inconsistent fasion, when he stumbled across a tweet from Aequabit essentially saying it'd be cool if we worked together and me dreaming of the day that that'd happen gladly accepted and then after a while we came up with the team name Chron1X and started to work on this website together.

Front-End Websites

I love experimenting with new styles for websites and just new ways to impress customers. I use HTML, CSS and JQuery most of the time when I am. Also sometimes I try to push the boundries by experimenting on forum softwares and frameworks like Bootstrap.

Back-End Websites

When I first discovered the brilliant language, PHP, I was intimidated, but once I broke it down it felt natural and I try to always challenge myself, trying to achieve bigger projects. I also know that Aequabit is a fan of it too!
